# Mission

## What We Do

Tuskrank provides pet owners with the data and intelligence they need to make informed decisions about pet food — replacing guesswork and marketing spin with transparent, searchable, and comparable product analysis.

## Who We Serve

**Primary users**: US pet owners who want to understand what they are feeding their pets and find better options.

**Secondary users**: Veterinarians, pet store owners, and pet food industry professionals who need reliable product data.

## How We Deliver Value

1. **Search**: Instantly find any pet food product, ingredient, or brand.
2. **Compare**: Side-by-side product comparisons on nutrition, ingredients, safety, and price.
3. **Analyze**: AI-powered ingredient breakdowns explaining what each component does, its quality grade, and potential risks.
4. **Discover**: Personalized alternative suggestions based on nutritional goals, dietary restrictions, and budget.
5. **Explain**: Plain-language explanations of pet nutrition concepts, regulatory standards, and labeling practices.

## What Makes Us Different

- **Data-first**: Every claim is backed by structured data, not subjective opinions.
- **AI-powered**: LLM-driven analysis makes complex nutritional science accessible.
- **US-focused**: Deep coverage of the US market, including FDA/AAFCO regulatory context.
- **Transparent methodology**: Our scoring and ranking algorithms are documented and defensible.

## Core Principle

Pets cannot choose their own food. The humans who care for them deserve the best possible tools to make that choice responsibly.
