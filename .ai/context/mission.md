## Statement

Tuskrank exists to **demystify pet food and ingredients** so that every pet owner in the United States can make confident, informed, and healthier choices for their animals.

## What We Do

We build a search and intelligence platform that combines:

- **Structured product data** — brands, products, ingredient panels, guarantees.
- **Ingredient-level analysis** — quality grading, controversy flags, sourcing notes.
- **AI-powered explanations** — readable, neutral, sourced insights.
- **Comparison tools** — side-by-side product comparisons.
- **Discovery** — healthier alternatives for any given product.

## Who We Serve

- US dog and cat owners
- New pet parents choosing a first food
- Pet owners switching foods for health reasons
- Pet owners managing allergies or sensitivities
- Multi-pet households

## What We Are NOT

- We are **not a retailer** — we never sell products.
- We are **not a vet** — we never replace veterinary advice.
- We are **not a marketing channel** — we never accept payment for rankings.
