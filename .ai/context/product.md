# Product

> _Placeholder — to be elaborated in Sprint 1._

## Core Surfaces

### 1. Search

- Pet food product search
- Ingredient search
- Brand search
- Free-text natural queries

### 2. Product Detail

- Full ingredient panel
- Guaranteed analysis
- Nutritional quality score
- Controversy flags
- Healthier alternatives
- AI-generated plain-language explanation

### 3. Comparison

- Side-by-side product comparison (up to 4)
- Ingredient diff
- Score diff
- Cost-per-day diff (placeholder)

### 4. Ingredient Detail

- What the ingredient is
- Why it is used in pet food
- Quality grades
- Concerns and controversies
- Common products containing it

### 5. Healthier Alternatives

- Generated for any indexed product
- Ranked by quality score improvement
- Filtered by life stage and species

### 6. AI Explanations

- Plain-language explanations of ingredient panels
- Citations to authoritative sources (where available)
- Tone: neutral, factual, owner-friendly

## User Tiers

| Tier        | Capabilities                                          |
| ----------- | ----------------------------------------------------- |
| Anonymous   | Search, view products, view ingredients, compare     |
| Authenticated | Save products, save comparisons, personalized feed  |
| Admin       | CMS for products, ingredients, brands, scoring rules |

## Non-Goals (Sprint 0)

- Selling products
- Replacing veterinary advice
- Affiliate commerce
- User-generated content (reviews)
