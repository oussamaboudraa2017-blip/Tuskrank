# Product

## Product Definition

Tuskrank is a Pet Food Search & Intelligence Platform. It is a web-based SaaS application serving the US market.

## Core Features

### 1. Product Search
- Full-text search across product names, brands, and categories.
- Filters by pet type (dog, cat), life stage, dietary need, price range.
- Autocomplete and suggestion UI.

### 2. Ingredient Search
- Search any ingredient to see which products contain it.
- Ingredient detail pages with nutritional function, safety profile, and quality tier.
- Cross-referencing with veterinary research.

### 3. Product Comparison
- Side-by-side comparison of up to 4 products.
- Comparison dimensions: guaranteed analysis, ingredient list, price per serving, Tuskrank score, recall history.

### 4. Ingredient Analysis
- AI-generated plain-language breakdown of every ingredient in a product.
- Ingredient quality grading (premium, standard, low, controversial).
- Flagging of potential allergens, fillers, and artificial additives.

### 5. Alternative Discovery
- Given a product, suggest healthier or better-value alternatives.
- Filtering by constraint (grain-free, limited ingredient, budget, etc.).

### 6. AI Explanations
- LLM-powered explanations of why a product scored a certain way.
- Conversational Q&A about any product or ingredient.
- Context-aware responses citing specific data points.

## User Flows

### Pet Owner Flow
1. Search for a product or ingredient.
2. View detailed product/ingredient page.
3. Compare with alternatives.
4. Read AI analysis.
5. Make an informed decision.

### Veterinarian Flow
1. Search for products meeting specific nutritional criteria.
2. Compare multiple products against AAFCO standards.
3. Export comparison data for client consultations.

## Non-Goals (V1)

- No e-commerce / purchasing.
- No user accounts (V1 is public-access).
- No mobile app (responsive web only).
- No social features (reviews, forums).
- No pet profile management (V1).
