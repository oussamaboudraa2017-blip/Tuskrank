# Vision

## The Problem

Pet owners in the United States face a crisis of information when choosing food for their pets. The $50B+ US pet food industry offers tens of thousands of products with opaque ingredient lists, misleading marketing claims, and no standardized way to evaluate quality. Owners are forced to rely on brand reputation, packaging aesthetics, or anecdotal advice — none of which reliably correlate with nutritional quality.

## The Vision

Tuskrank will become the definitive source of truth for pet food intelligence in the United States. We envision a world where every pet owner can, in seconds, understand exactly what is in their pet's food, how it compares to alternatives, and whether it meets their pet's specific health needs — all powered by transparent data and AI-driven analysis.

## Long-Term Ambition

- **Comprehensive coverage**: Every commercially available pet food product in the US market indexed and analyzed.
- **Real-time intelligence**: Ingredient safety alerts, recall notifications, and formula change tracking.
- **Personalized nutrition**: AI-powered recommendations tailored to individual pet profiles (age, breed, health conditions, allergies).
- **Industry transparency**: Forcing accountability on manufacturers by making product data publicly comparable.

## Success Metrics

- Index 50,000+ products within the first year.
- Serve 1M+ monthly active users within 18 months.
- Become the top-ranking result for "pet food review" and "pet food comparison" queries.
- Be cited by veterinarians and pet health publications as a trusted data source.
