## Long-Term Vision

Tuskrank aims to become the **default pet food intelligence platform** for the US market — a single source of truth for pet owners who want to understand, compare, and choose better food for their animals.

We envision a future where:

- Every pet owner can answer the question _"Is this food actually good for my pet?"_ in under 30 seconds.
- Ingredient quality is transparent, comparable, and explainable.
- Pet food discovery is **search-first**, not marketing-first.
- AI augments decisions without replacing owner judgment.

## Aspirational Scale

- Millions of monthly active users across the US.
- Hundreds of thousands of indexed products and ingredients.
- Sub-100ms search response times worldwide.
- Sub-1.0% monthly churn on retained users.

## North-Star Metric

> **Weekly Active Pet Owners (WAU) who complete a comparison or health check.**
