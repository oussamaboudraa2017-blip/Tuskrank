# Sprint 4 — Scoring

> _Placeholder sprint spec._

## Goals

- Author ingredient quality grading model.

## Acceptance Criteria

- Scores are explainable (each input contributes a known weight).
- Alternatives are restricted to same species + life stage.
- Scoring pipeline produces deterministic outputs for identical inputs.
